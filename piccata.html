Chicken Piccata

Preparation Time: 50 minutes

As Prepared By:Gordon Ramsay

Sections

  • Prepared By
  • Main Description
  • Steps
  • Additional Details
  • Video Description
Description:Chicken Piccata is an Italian dish featuring chicken breasts dredged in flour, pan-fried, and served with a zesty lemon and caper sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Capers

    • Steps:

    1. Dredge chicken in flour and pan-fry until golden.
    2. Make a sauce with butter, lemon, and capers.
    3. Pour the sauce over the chicken.
    Additional Details

    Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with pasta or rice.
